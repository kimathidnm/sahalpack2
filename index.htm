Products and Services

PP White labels & stickers

PP white labels are made from Polypropylene, a material that is tear proof, water proof and durable. It is also resistant to fading and discoloration that is often characteristic of standard paper labels.

PP Clear labels & Stickers

PP Clear labels are a transparent format of Polypropylene (PP) material. They provides a cost-competitive, sustainable solution and delivers higher performance and easier processing versus other transparent materials.

Semi-gloss laminated labels

Semi-Gloss lamination produces a shiny, glass-like appearance on the label that enhances the colour and vibrancy of the ink on a page. The labels are waterproof, and therefore can be wiped clean and dry if lightly splashed with water.

PVC labels

PVC label are made from high-quality materials and are assured to not fade or get damaged. They are popularly used on bottle water and other industrial use.

BOPP/CPP bread bags

The BOPP/CPP bread bags are ideal for small, medium and largescale bakery & confectionary products, with flexographic printing up to 8 colors.

Thermal material

Thermal label materials are made for general purpose labelling products such as price tags and names of products.

 

Kraft paper bags

We manufacture Standard virgin brown paper bags from Kraft paper. These are ideal for industrial as well as home packaging of products.

BOPP wrap around labels

Wrap around labels are roll fed labels available in oriented poly propylene in pearlised white, opaque or natural transparent
